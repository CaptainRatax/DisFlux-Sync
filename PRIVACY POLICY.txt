Privacy Policy for DisFLux Sync

This Privacy Policy explains how DisFLux Sync collects, uses, processes, and retains information when the bot is used on Discord and Fluxer.

DisFLux Sync is operated by Captain Ratax.

By using DisFLux Sync, you acknowledge this Privacy Policy.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the use of DisFLux Sync and any related support or operational services made available by the bot owner.

2. Information We Collect

DisFLux Sync is designed to minimize data retention.

The bot may collect and process limited technical identifiers and configuration data required to provide its synchronization features, including:

- Server IDs
- Channel IDs
- Role IDs
- User IDs
- Link configuration data, such as selected sync priority and channel sync options
- Temporary message identifiers when technically required to support synchronization features such as replies, message edits, message deletions, and reactions

3. Information We Do Not Retain

DisFLux Sync does not permanently store private server content.

The bot does not permanently retain:

- Message content
- Channel names
- Channel topics or descriptions
- Usernames, display names, or nicknames
- Role names
- Attachments or shared files
- Full copies of server structures or conversations
- Message histories

Where real-time synchronization requires temporary processing of content, embeds, or attachments, such data is processed in memory for transmission purposes and is not retained as persistent private content.

4. Real-Time Processing

To provide synchronization features, DisFLux Sync may temporarily process message content, embeds, attachments, role updates, nickname updates, mentions, and other supported events in real time.

This processing is performed solely for the purpose of delivering the requested synchronization functionality between linked Discord and Fluxer servers.

5. How We Use Information

DisFLux Sync uses the limited information it processes and stores to:

- Link Discord and Fluxer servers
- Link channels, roles, and users
- Mirror supported events between linked servers in real time
- Maintain minimal technical mappings necessary for synchronization features
- Operate, secure, maintain, and improve the bot
- Investigate abuse, misuse, service issues, or security incidents
- Comply with legal obligations where applicable

6. Data Retention

DisFLux Sync retains only the minimum persistent data necessary to operate the service.

This generally includes identifier-based mappings and configuration settings until:

- The linked configuration is removed
- The bot is removed from the relevant servers
- The stored mapping is no longer required for the operation of the service
- The operator determines that deletion is appropriate for operational, legal, or security reasons

Temporary message identifier mappings used for synchronization mechanics may be retained for a limited period and may expire automatically.

7. Data Sharing

DisFLux Sync does not sell personal information.

DisFLux Sync does not share private server content with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Information may be disclosed only in the following limited circumstances:

- When required by applicable law, regulation, court order, or lawful request from a competent authority
- When reasonably necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of the bot owner, users, or others
- When required to investigate fraud, abuse, security incidents, or misuse of the service

8. International Processing

Because Discord, Fluxer, hosting providers, or related technical infrastructure may operate internationally, technical data processed by DisFLux Sync may pass through systems located in different jurisdictions.

9. Data Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are taken to protect stored data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration.

However, no system can guarantee absolute security, and DisFLux Sync cannot guarantee that unauthorized access, loss, or misuse will never occur.

10. Third-Party Platforms

DisFLux Sync operates on third-party platforms, including Discord and Fluxer. Your use of those platforms is also governed by their own terms, privacy practices, and policies.

DisFLux Sync is not responsible for the independent privacy or security practices of Discord, Fluxer, or other third-party services.

11. Your Choices

Server administrators control whether to link servers and whether to configure channel synchronization, including optional synchronization of other bot messages and webhook messages.

If you no longer want DisFLux Sync to process data in a server, the bot should be removed and any relevant links or configurations should be deleted.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

If material changes are made, the updated version will become effective when published with a new effective date.

Your continued use of DisFLux Sync after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised Privacy Policy.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, legal requests, or privacy-related concerns, contact the operator using the public contact details made available for DisFLux Sync.
